About Me

I'm CJ, a writer and content creator from Scotland with a passion for storytelling in all its forms. Whether it's analyzing the latest film releases, reviewing fantasy novels, or crafting my own stories, I'm always exploring the narratives that shape our world.

On my YouTube channel, I create video essays and discussions about film, books, and games. I'm also working on my own fantasy book series, with the first novel currently in development.

When I'm not creating content, you'll find me reading, watching films, or exploring the Scottish countryside for inspiration.
